The Coastal Standard

Established in Florida, 2026

Crafted for the
Sunshine State

LuxeThread Florida was born from a simple observation: streetwear often lacks the breathability required for the coast, and coastal wear often lacks the edge of the street.

We bridge that gap. Using premium Pima cotton, ventilated technical meshes, and sustainable linens, we create garments that thrive in 90-degree humidity while maintaining a silhouette that belongs in the world's most exclusive lounges.

01

Precision

Every stitch is reinforced for longevity. Our garments are built to endure the salt air and the city grind.

02

Exclusivity

We release in small batches. Once a "Combo" configuration is retired, it never returns to the vault.

03

Community

Based in Florida, we support local artisans and photographers who capture the true essence of our home.
